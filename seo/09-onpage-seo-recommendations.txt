SECTION 5: COMPETITOR GAPS TO EXPLOIT
================================================================================

Based on analysis of listed competitors:

5.1 DROPPA gaps: Strong nationally but weaker on Cape Town local SEO.
    CapeLoad should dominate suburb-level keywords.

5.2 PICUP gaps: Focused on enterprise/API. CapeLoad can win individual
    consumer searches (bakkie hire, single item moves).

5.3 LALAMOVE gaps: Relatively new in SA market. CapeLoad can position
    as the local Cape Town alternative.

5.4 THE COURIER GUY gaps: Strong brand but expensive for large items.
    CapeLoad should target "cheap bakkie hire" and "affordable movers".

5.5 MR DELIVERY (MrD) gaps: Primarily food delivery. CapeLoad can
    capture "goods delivery" and "furniture delivery" searches that
    MrD doesn't serve.

5.6 UBER CONNECT gaps: Limited vehicle types. CapeLoad should emphasize
    multi-vehicle (6+ types) advantage in all comparisons.

5.7 GENERAL gap: None of the competitors have strong suburb-level
    landing pages. Building 15-20 area pages will give CapeLoad
    a massive local SEO advantage in Cape Town.

5.8 GENERAL gap: Few competitors have comparison pages. Being first
    to create "CapeLoad vs X" pages means CapeLoad controls the
    narrative when people search for alternatives.

5.9 GENERAL gap: Afrikaans keywords are almost completely ignored by
    all competitors. Creating a few Afrikaans-friendly pages or
    blog posts can capture an untapped audience.

5.10 GENERAL gap: Driver acquisition keywords are underserved.
     Content targeting "earn with your bakkie" and "delivery driver
     jobs cape town" has low competition but high intent.
